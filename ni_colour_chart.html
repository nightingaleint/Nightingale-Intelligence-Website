Palette philosophy

The NI colour system uses a shared dark ink background across all platforms, with each platform given a unique accent family:

· NI Parent — deep teal + salmon. Cool intelligence with warm humanity.
· High Horse — seafoam/forest green. Environmental data, alive and growing. Keep as-is.
· TWN — navy + lavender/lilac/blush. Travel, night skies, feminine softness (She Travels) balanced with cool aviation blues.
· Vault — obsidian + gold/silver/pearl. Wealth, security, quiet prestige. Metallic and cool.
· Living — forest green + teal + terracotta/sandstone. Earth, nature, organic architecture. The warmest and most grounded platform.

Shared rule: text is never close to the background in value. Minimum contrast ratio target: 4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for decorative/label text.
